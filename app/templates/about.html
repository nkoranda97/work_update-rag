About Email‑RAG

How It Works

Email‑RAG is a powerful tool that helps you search and analyze your email content using retrieval‑augmented generation (RAG). It blends vector search with generative AI to surface the most relevant snippets and craft concise answers.

Using the Search Interface

Question input

Enter your question in the text area. Be specific about what you're looking for—for example:

  • "Summarize Andy's trimer work in 2020"
  • "What were the key points from the last team meeting?"
  • "Find all discussions about project deadlines"

Filters

  • Sender: Filter results to specific email senders
  • Date range: Limit results to a specific time period

Top‑k results slider

The k slider controls how many email snippets are considered:

  • Lower values (1‑10): Precise, focused answers
  • Higher values (11‑50): Broader coverage for complex topics

Tips for better results

  • Be specific in your questions
  • Use filters when you know the context
  • Start with a lower k value and increase if you need more context
  • Review the snippets to understand how the answer was generated
